Tell Me More!

About


This special track on the synergy between Urban Informatics and Large Language Models is a pioneering event at GSCS & ICUI 2025. It brings together experts to share recent challenges and opportunities, aiming to inspire future research. With oral presentations, the special track provides a platform for researchers from urban informatics, large language models, smart city, AI, spatial data management, big data and data science to exchange ideas, exchange knowledge, and explore the latest advancements in these fields. By including participants from diverse domains, the special track aims to connect practical applications with research, encouraging collaboration that will shape the future of urban informatics and large language models. The special track will feature keynote speeches from leading experts, providing insights into the latest innovations and breakthroughs in the field, which serves as a beacon for current and future research. This special track will bridge the researchers and practitioners in the areas of urban informatics and large language models, providing a communication platform.

Submission Guidelines


  • Abstract (200-300 words): submitted to https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=gscsicui2025. Authors should select "Special Session: LLM Powered Urban Informatics" in the Easychair system during submission.

  • Full paper: The ICUI conference has established several awards, including First Prize, Second Prize, Third Prize, and Merit-based awards for outstanding papers. To compete for the Best Conference Paper Award, please submit your full paper to https://www.springer.com/journal/44212 and make sure to select the special issue "Global Smart Cities Summit (GSCS 2025) cum The 4th International Conference on Urban Informatics (ICUI 2025)" during submission. After review, your paper will have the opportunity to win an award and be officially published in the Urban Informatics journal.
