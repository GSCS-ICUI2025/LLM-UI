About


This special track on the synergy between Urban Informatics and Large Language Models is a pioneering event at GSCS & ICUI 2025. It brings together experts to share recent challenges and opportunities, aiming to inspire future research. With oral presentations, the special track provides a platform for researchers from urban informatics, large language models, smart city, AI, spatial data management, big data and data science to exchange ideas, exchange knowledge, and explore the latest advancements in these fields. By including participants from diverse domains, the special track aims to connect practical applications with research, encouraging collaboration that will shape the future of urban informatics and large language models. The special track will feature keynote speeches from leading experts, providing insights into the latest innovations and breakthroughs in the field, which serves as a beacon for current and future research. This special track will bridge the researchers and practitioners in the areas of urban informatics and large language models, providing a communication platform.

Topics of Interest


  • Integration of Large Language Models in Urban Planning
  • Smart City Applications Powered by AI
  • Predictive Modeling for Urban Growth Using LLMs
  • Enhancing Urban Mobility with AI and LLMs
  • Multi-Modal Data Fusion for Urban Informatics
  • Real-Time Urban Monitoring with LLMs
  • AI for Disaster Management in Urban Areas
  • Urban Energy Optimization Using Machine Learning
  • Social Media Analysis for Urban Trends
  • Ethical Considerations in AI-Powered Urban Informatics
  • AI for Sustainable Urban Development
  • Data Privacy and Security in Urban AI Systems

Important Dates


  • Abstract Submission deadline: 1 July 2025

  • Submission of full paper (for award eligibility): 15 July 2025

Submission Guidelines


  • Abstract (200-300 words): submitted to here. Authors should select "Special Session: LLM Powered Urban Informatics" in the Easychair system during submission.

  • Full paper: The ICUI conference has established several awards, including First Prize, Second Prize, Third Prize, and Merit-based awards for outstanding papers. To compete for the Best Conference Paper Award, please submit your full paper to here and make sure to select the special issue "Global Smart Cities Summit (GSCS 2025) cum The 4th International Conference on Urban Informatics (ICUI 2025)" during submission. After review, your paper will have the opportunity to win an award and be officially published in the Urban Informatics journal.

  • Abstracts and full papers (for award eligibility) are accepted.
